    IIIT Dharwad


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information Technology Dharwad is one of the 20 IIITs proposed under non-profit, Public-Private Partnership model set up by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India. It is an academic and research institute funded by the Government of India, Government of Karnataka and industrial partner Keonics. Recently, the institute has been declared as an Institute of National Importance under the Indian Institutes of Information Technology Public-Private Partnership Act of Parliament (No. 23 of 2017).

    STUDENT LIFE

    Life @IIITDWD is ‘Adventure every day’. It is just the hard work of earlier batches that the institute right now has 20 fully functioning clubs, committees and societies. 10 sports clubs, 6 cultural clubs and 4 technical clubs are flourishing in the wonderful ambience of the institute. The new massive campus is well equipped with all the facilities required for overall growth of the students. Institute has consistently grabbed attention in various national level competitions and is aspiring for more success. Want to witness progress? Welcome to IIIT Dharwad!

    Computer Science

    The Institute offers B.Tech and Doctoral degrees. With areas of research as Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cryptography & Network Security, Computer Networks and Algorithms

    Electrical Engineering

    The Institute offers B.Tech and Doctoral degrees. With areas of research as Chip Designing (VLSI), Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS), Signal and Speech Processing, Communication, Robotics, Sensors and Internet of Things

      NEWS & UPDATES

      Traditional day and Matrubhasha Divas

      Our Tradition is what defines us. IIIT Dharwad celebrated the TRADITIONAL DAY & MATRUBHASHA DIVAS on 25th February 2020, cherishing one's culture. Both the faculty and students came forward with innovative ideas making it a Memorable day. Several skits threw light on most sensitive topics of our society such as Sexual Harassment, and many speeches were delivered by pupils in their native languages. All in all this day has now become a crucial one in the legacy of our college.

      Convocation 2019 with gaiety and gusto

      The first convocation ceremony of IIIT Dharwad was commemorated with a lot of zeal in August 2019. The first batch had started its endeavour in the academic year of 2015. Despite being only the first batch of the college the students provided the college with a lot of recognition and for that, the college is eternally grateful to them. The Director and all the teachers extended warm greetings to them and wished them luck for their future ahead.
